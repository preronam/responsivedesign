About Me


Prerona is currently the Managing Director of Professional Theatre and Dance Youth Academy (PTDYA), a non-profit organization providing arts education to Chicago schools in under-resourced commmunities. She oversees all fundraising and development initiatives for PTDYA. Prerona has a backgroud in Communications and recently graduated from the University of Chicago with a Master's in Literature. She has a love for the literary arts and enjoys listening to classic rock.
